How It Works

Deliver.

FLWs deliver proven, high-impact services — from Child Health Campaigns to Kangaroo Mother Care — through trusted, locally-led organizations.

Delivery

Services at the Last Mile

Powered by CommCare

Frontline workers use CommCare — Dimagi's proven mobile platform — for structured data collection during every service interaction. Guided workflows ensure consistent, high-quality delivery across all programs.

Offline-First Design

Services are delivered in areas with limited or no connectivity. CommCare works fully offline, capturing every data point locally and syncing automatically when a connection becomes available.

Syncs When Connected

Data flows seamlessly from the field to the platform. When workers regain connectivity, all service records sync to the cloud — ensuring nothing is lost and real-time dashboards stay current.

Data

Every Interaction Captured

Real-time data from the frontline

Every service delivery interaction generates structured data — who was served, what service was provided, when and where it happened. This data flows in real time to program managers and funders, creating unprecedented transparency.

No paper forms. No manual data entry. No delays. From the moment a worker completes a service, the data is captured digitally and ready for verification.

Structured workflows at scale

  • Guided protocols — Workers follow step-by-step workflows that ensure every service meets quality standards, regardless of location or experience level.
  • 80+ countries — The same delivery infrastructure has been proven across diverse contexts, from urban clinics to remote rural communities.
  • Multimedia capture — Photos, GPS coordinates, timestamps, and biometric data are collected alongside service records for verification.
  • Supervisor oversight — Program managers monitor delivery in real time, identifying gaps and supporting workers where needed.
