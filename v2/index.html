The Gap

Billions flow into global health each year. But how much reaches the frontline?

Funders commit resources to improve health outcomes in communities that need it most. But without verified data, there is no way to confirm services were actually delivered. Workers go uncompensated. Data stays fragmented. The gap between funding and frontline impact remains invisible.

How CommCare Connect Works: Learn, Deliver, Verify and Pay

Learn

Workers select training through a digital platform integrated with CommCare. They build skills at their own pace, preparing for frontline service delivery.

→
Deliver

Frontline workers deliver health services using CommCare for data collection and workflow management. Every interaction is captured.

→
Verify

Biometric verification, GPS tracking, and algorithmic validation confirm that services were actually delivered. Not self-reported. Not estimated. Verified.

→
Pay

Workers receive fair, transparent compensation managed end-to-end on the platform. No hidden deductions. No unexplained delays. Full visibility.

→
