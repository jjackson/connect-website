Accountability

Funding Accountability, Built In

You invest in health outcomes. You deserve to see them verified. Connect traces every dollar from commitment through verified delivery, connecting funders with a network of trusted, locally-led organizations (LLOs) to deliver proven, high-impact services.

No more relying on self-reported data or delayed evaluations. Every service interaction is captured, validated through biometric and GPS verification, and linked back to the funding that made it possible. From Child Health Campaigns (CHC) to Kangaroo Mother Care (KMC) and Early Childhood Development (ECD), the result is a continuous, auditable record of what your investment achieved.
