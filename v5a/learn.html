Learn

Frontline Workers use a mobile app to build skills at their own pace, preparing for high-impact service delivery.

How Learning Works

Training That Meets Workers Where They Are

Mobile-First Training

Workers access training modules on their phones, learning skills for health service delivery through interactive content and assessments.

Self-Paced Progress

Each worker moves through modules at their own speed, with pre-tests and post-tests measuring knowledge gained.

Locally Relevant Content

Training materials are localized and adapted for each intervention and region, ensuring cultural relevance and practical applicability.

Curriculum

What Workers Learn

  • Health service protocols (e.g., KMC positioning, CHC screening procedures)
  • Data collection and form completion via CommCare
  • Safety and infection prevention practices
  • Danger sign recognition and referral pathways
  • Community engagement and counseling techniques

Structured Learning Modules

Interactive, assessment-based training designed for frontline health workers

The Connect Advantage

Quality From Day One

Digital training through Connect means FLWs don't need classroom time. They learn on their own devices, prove readiness through assessments, and only begin delivering services once they've demonstrated competence. This ensures quality from day one.
