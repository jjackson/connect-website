The Model

Learn. Deliver. Verify. Pay.

Four steps that connect funding to frontline impact.

01
Step 01

Learn

Frontline workers access digital mobile training, building skills at their own pace through structured modules designed by health experts and validated by local partners.

02
Step 02

Deliver

Workers deliver health services in their communities using CommCare, a mobile app that guides each visit with structured protocols and captures real-time data.

03
Step 03

Verify

Every service is validated through biometric confirmation, GPS location data, and algorithmic checks, ensuring funders can trust that outcomes are real.

04
Step 04

Pay

Verified results trigger transparent mobile money payments directly to frontline workers, creating a sustainable incentive tied to real impact.
