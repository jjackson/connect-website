The LDVP cycle

Four surfaces. One platform.

Every program on Connect inherits the same four-surface architecture. Learn trains the worker. Deliver moves them through a microplanned grid. Verify proves the visit happened. Pay settles what was earned.

01 — Learn

Train any FLW, anywhere

Self-paced digital learning plus an AI coach built on Open Chat Studio. Validated with ~400 FLWs.
02 — Deliver

Activate local partners

Trial Run contracting de-risks new LLOs. 37 LLOs onboarded under the model.
03 — Verify

Four layers of proof

Biometric, GPS, photo, algorithmic. We paid people to beat it. They couldn't.
04 — Pay

Performance-based payment

FLWs paid per verified visit. LLOs paid on delivery milestones. No middle layer.
