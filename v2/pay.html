Transparent From Earning to Delivery

Frontline Workers (FLWs) see their full payment cycle: how earnings are calculated, when payments are processed, and exactly what arrives. No hidden deductions. No unexplained delays. Full visibility from the moment a service is delivered to the moment compensation reaches a worker's hands.

Return on investment

Proven Returns

The Economic Multiplier

When Frontline Workers (FLWs) are paid for results, not effort, the impact multiplies. The Financing Alliance for Health demonstrated a $10 return for every $1 invested in community health workers. Paying for results isn't just ethical — it's economically powerful.

Paying FLWs what they deserve strengthens the entire health system. It improves retention, drives higher quality service delivery, and builds the trust that communities depend on.

The Process

How Payments Work

Step 1

Earnings Calculated

Compensation is based on verified results, not estimates or self-reports. FLWs can see exactly how each amount is determined, tied directly to the impactful work they completed.

Step 2

Payment Processed

Payments are managed end-to-end on the platform. No intermediary delays. No funds lost in transit. The entire process is tracked and transparent to everyone involved.

Step 3

Worker Receives

Compensation goes direct to the FLW, with full transparency on the amount and timing. Workers know what to expect and when to expect it — no surprises, no confusion.
