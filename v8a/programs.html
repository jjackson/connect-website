The programs are
the proof of the platform.

Ten programs currently run on Connect. Four have enough evidence to merit their own write-up. Six are in early deployment or coming online. Each inherits the LDVP architecture; each runs its own cost curve; each adds learnings back to the platform.

Active programs with detail pages
Child Health Campaigns

CHC

Nutrition screening (MUAC), treatment referral, childhood vaccination. 44 LLOs. 880K+ visits in 14 months. The program that informed most of what Connect is today.
Kangaroo Mother Care

KMC

Small & vulnerable newborn care. 5 structured home visits per SVN. Aligned to WHO 2022 guidelines. Active in India with mortality-reduction targets.
Early Childhood Development

ECD

Responsive caregiving, talk-and-play, behavioral rehearsal. Malawi pilot moved caregiver knowledge +33-41% and observed behavior +21%. Expanding to 8-10 visits.
Reading Glasses

Readers

Presbyopia screening and reading glasses distribution with Restoring Vision. A new sector for Connect; same LDVP scaffolding; Trial Run model applied.
