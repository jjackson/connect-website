Paying for verified results, not projected effort.

Connect is a platform that binds funding to delivery. Frontline workers learn, deliver, and are verified through the same system, then paid on mobile money when the work is confirmed. Built by Dimagi with a network of locally-led organizations across 11 countries.

stakeholders // chain

Three stakeholders. One aligned chain.

role=funder

Funders

Every dollar ties to a verified delivery with GPS, timestamps, and image capture. Cost per verified outcome becomes a real metric, not an estimate. Microplanning ensures coverage reaches the households that get skipped.

role=llo

Locally-Led Organizations

Sustainable, performance-based contracts with the verification, training, and payment infrastructure provided. Scale up or down against actual demand. Use the same data as proof for your next grant application.

role=flw

Frontline Workers

Learn new skills digitally on your phone. Deliver meaningful services in your own community. Get paid directly on mobile money when your work is verified. No intermediaries, no delayed payments.
