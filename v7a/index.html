The cycle

Four steps. One closed loop. Evidence at every stage.

Workers learn before they deliver. Every service is verified before it is paid. The platform turns that sequence into infrastructure — repeatable across programs, countries, and partners.

01 · Learn

Digital training with a pass bar.

Self-paced, offline-capable modules. FLWs must pass an in-app assessment before they can deliver.
02 · Deliver

Locally-led, app-guided service.

44 LLOs across 11 countries. Workflows captured step-by-step with GPS and timestamps.
03 · Verify

Biometric, GPS, algorithmic.

Four independent layers. Fraud risk detectable with AUC 0.91 from three data elements alone.
04 · Pay

Mobile money on verification.

Cost per verified visit dropped from $2.20 to $1.70 — leaner at scale, not more bloated.

Not a collection of stats. A working theory of how services actually get delivered.

Each step below hides a decade of arguments we have had with ourselves: what counts as training, what counts as coverage, what counts as fraud, and what counts as payment. Click into any one to read the long version.

Who it's for

Three stakeholders. One set of aligned incentives.

Funders

A transparent, cost-effective way to invest in verified outcomes. Every dollar ties to a delivered service, with GPS, biometrics, and photo capture. Microplanning ensures equitable coverage — and cost per verified outcome drops as the network grows.

Locally-Led Organizations

Access to sustainable funding and the digital infrastructure to deliver it well. Performance-based contracts let you scale without adding overhead. Verification, payment, and oversight are handled by the platform — you bring the community relationships.

Frontline Workers

Learn new skills on your own phone. Deliver meaningful services in your community. Get paid directly via mobile money for verified work — no intermediaries, no delayed paperwork, no disputes.
