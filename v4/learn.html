How It Works: Step 1

Learn.

Frontline Workers use a mobile app to build skills at their own pace, preparing for high-impact service delivery.

Training

Training That Meets Workers
Where They Are

Workers select training modules directly on their mobile phones -- no classroom required. Connect's learning platform is designed for low-bandwidth environments and basic smartphones, so workers in even the most remote communities can access the skills they need. Modules are short, practical, and built around the specific services workers will deliver in the field.

Outcomes

From Knowledge to Action

Training on Connect is not just about learning -- it is the gateway to real job opportunities. Once a worker completes a training module and demonstrates competency, they become eligible for funded service delivery assignments. Every skill gained maps directly to a service that communities need.

Self-Paced

Workers learn on their own schedule, fitting training around family and community responsibilities without losing income.

Mobile-First

Designed for basic Android smartphones and low-bandwidth networks. No laptop, no Wi-Fi, no problem.

Skill Verification

Competency is confirmed before workers enter the field, ensuring quality service delivery from day one.
