How It Works

Pay.

Every Frontline Worker is paid for their impactful work. Paying for results, not effort.

Transparency

Transparent From Earning to Delivery

Frontline workers see the full payment cycle -- from the moment a task is completed and verified to the instant funds arrive. Every step is visible, every calculation is clear.

No hidden deductions. No opaque formulas. Workers know exactly what they earned, why they earned it, and when they will be paid. This is not an incentive program -- it is fair compensation for verified work.

Full transparency builds trust and motivates workers to deliver quality services, knowing they will be recognized and compensated fairly.

The Economic Multiplier
$10:1
Return on Investment

Fair compensation is not just the right thing to do -- it is economically powerful. Every dollar paid to a frontline worker generates an estimated tenfold return in community health outcomes, economic activity, and system-level savings.

Payment Flow

How Payments Work

1

Earnings Calculated

As verified service deliveries are confirmed, earnings are calculated in real time. Workers see exactly how much they have earned and for which tasks -- no guesswork, no surprises.

2

Payment Processed

Payments are batched and processed through secure mobile money channels. The entire transaction is logged and auditable, giving funders and managers full visibility into where funds flow.

3

Worker Receives

Funds arrive directly to the worker's mobile wallet. Workers receive a confirmation with a full breakdown of their earnings -- no intermediaries, no delays, no deductions.
