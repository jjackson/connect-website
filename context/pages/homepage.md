## How It Works — The LDVP Flow
Four steps that form a complete cycle:

1. **Learn** — FLWs complete digital training and must pass an assessment before delivering services
2. **Deliver** — FLWs deliver proven services guided by a mobile app; LLOs manage local operations
3. **Verify** — Biometric, GPS, and algorithmic verification confirm every service
4. **Pay** — Mobile money payments flow directly to workers upon verification

Each step links to its own detail page.

## Who It's For (Audiences)
Three distinct value props:

### Funders
A transparent, cost-effective way to invest in verified outcomes. Every dollar ties to a delivered service. Microplanning ensures equitable coverage. Cost per verified outcome drops as the network scales.

### Locally-Led Organizations
Access to sustainable funding and the digital tools to deliver it well. Performance-based contracts let you scale without adding overhead. The platform handles verification, payment, and oversight infrastructure.

### Frontline Workers
Learn new skills digitally. Deliver meaningful services in your community. Get paid directly via mobile money for verified work — no intermediaries, no delays.

## Content Routing Strategy (for generator)
The homepage doesn't own the deep content — it surfaces the most compelling stats and narrative hooks from each sub-page. Every LDVP step gets one teaser stat. Every audience gets one-sentence value prop. Programs are shown in catalog form, not detailed.

The homepage's job: **draw visitors into the detail pages, where the real evidence lives.**
