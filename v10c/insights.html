Methodology — what makes the cut

An entry appears on this page only if (a) the underlying claim is scoped to a program or to a clearly-labelled platform mechanism; (b) any number is paired with a denominator, an evaluation N, or a comparator; and (c) any in-progress finding is named with the evaluation that is currently underway. Generic stats without a benchmark are excluded by policy. Entries scoped to a program (CHC, KMC, ECD, Readers) are listed under that program's group; entries scoped to a Connect-wide mechanism are listed under Platform. Every entry carries an evidence-tier badge — hard data for measured findings with a named report and N; triangulated for findings with multiple independent sources or external benchmark; hypothesis for in-validation findings with the evaluation named. We do not soft-tag claims with no source — they are dropped.
