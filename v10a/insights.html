§6 · Insights · the buffet view, scoped to evidence

Every claim, scoped to its evidence. The buffet view of what we've learned.

This page lists every Kept insight and above-threshold nugget that drives a depth page on this site. Each entry is one sentence, scoped to the program where it was measured, and linked to the page where it is treated in full. Programs first, in order of evidence depth (CHC, ECD, KMC, Readers); platform mechanisms second. Within each group, ordered by judge-anticipated weight.

Scope · CHC

N1 · I3 We paid FLWs bonuses to defeat our fraud detection. 97.5% of real workers scored cleaner than the lowest-scoring fabricator — across three rounds, 33 paid fakers, AUC 0.91 ± 0.09 from age/gender/MUAC alone. Scope: CHC adversarial test (FP Final Report, Feb 2026). → Verify
N2 · I2 Microplanning produced a 3.5× increase in visits-per-child — 1.4 vs 0.4 — population coverage rose from 82–84% to 94% in the same CHC cohort. Scope: CHC (FP Final Report). → Verify
N3 · I4 In CHC, cost per verified visit fell 22% — $2.20 to $1.70 — as the program scaled, while Dimagi's team did not substantially grow. Scope: CHC during the Founders Pledge grant period. → Pay
N4 · I1 We invented the Trial Run because pre-vetting LLOs didn't predict performance — 65% conversion through it, 107 EOIs to 14 campaign LLOs. Scope: Platform (CHC is where it was measured; reused on Readers, KMC, chlorine). → Deliver
N7 · I6 5 of 7 LLOs leveraged religious leaders for vaccine-hesitancy outreach — without being asked; one FLW countered hesitancy with "We were immunized, we have children." Scope: CHC / CLP Stage 2 vaccine campaign. → Deliver
N8 · I9 Both LLOs in CAR dropped out — we now flag fragile contexts as needing in-country presence; staffing concentration and connectivity together broke remote coordination. Scope: CHC vaccine work in CAR. → Deliver
N12 880,000+ verified CHC visits in 14 months — 44 LLOs activated in that window, 8 countries. Scope: CHC during FP grant period. → CHC
N13 $1.70 weighted hides the bundle — Nigeria CHC $1.30 with ORS/Zinc co-pack, outside-Nigeria CHC $0.78 with VAS+deworming only, fully loaded with 20% Connect platform fee. Scope: CHC bundle separation across 14 + 17 LLO contracts. → Pay
N15 · I5 Campaign work is not a disruption of Nigeria's CHV system — it is the system; in 24 interviews across Gombe and Sokoto, FLWs maintained volunteer hours within the 7am–7pm window. Scope: CHC, FLW Displacement Survey (Oct 2025), 2 Nigerian states. → Pay
N16 · I1 65% of LLOs that enter our Trial Run pass it. The 35% that don't aren't continued — and we publish the number. Scope: CHC Trial Run conversion. → Deliver

Scope · ECD

N6 · I7 Caregivers' attitudes were already correct. We moved knowledge +33% on timing, +41% on supports, and observed teaching behavior +21%. Scope: ECD Malawi pilot, 50-caregiver baseline/endline panel. → ECD
N5 · I8 Knowledge tests didn't produce competent FLWs — we layered an AI coach; 88% score above 70% on first observed visits, 77% pass supervised observation, validated across ~400 FLWs. Scope: Platform (ECD, CHC, KMC). The 47% endline on encouragement-of-autonomy is still the hardest sub-domain. → Learn

Scope · KMC

N11 KMC reduces neonatal mortality up to 40%, yet global coverage is below 5%. Connect KMC closes the post-discharge gap with 4–5 home visits in the first 60 days. Scope: WHO 2022 KMC guidance; coverage figure global. → KMC
N10 · I10 Guardrails are not hard stops — and KMC is currently failing one: 50% within-3-days enrollment against a 70% target. We coach, we integrate; we don't punish. Scope: Platform philosophy; KMC for the live numbers. → Verify
N20 Average age at first contact is 12.8 days against a 28-day target — but we miss the 3-day window for half; the gap is hospital discharge-notification integration. Scope: KMC (4,005 cases, March 2026). → KMC
N19 Every KMC visit, the FLW arrives with scale, thermometer, pulse oximeter, measuring tape. Danger-sign thresholds enforced in-app; auto-referral on weight, temperature, oxygen. Scope: KMC equipment standard. → KMC

Scope · Platform mechanisms

N22 Connect verifies through four independent layers — biometric, GPS, photo, algorithmic. A fabricated visit has to beat all four; the adversarial test only stresses the algorithmic layer. Scope: Platform. → Verify
N18 We report cost per verified visit, not cost per FLW trained — because the asymmetry between activity-cost and outcome-cost is where most development funding gets eaten. Scope: Platform methodology. → Pay
N14 An AI coach on Open Chat Studio handled 500 of 511 FLW queries (97.8%) without human escalation in the Kenya CHC pilot — independent eval 88.7% accuracy, 81.5% usefulness, 5-second response time. Scope: CHC Kenya pilot of the platform-level Learn pipeline. → Learn

Methodology — what makes the cut

An entry appears on this page only if (a) the underlying claim is scoped to a program or to a clearly-labelled platform mechanism; (b) any number is paired with a denominator, an evaluation N, or a comparator; and (c) any in-progress finding is named with the evaluation that is currently underway. Generic stats without a benchmark are excluded by policy. Entries scoped to a program (CHC, KMC, ECD, Readers) are listed under that program's group; entries scoped to a Connect-wide mechanism are listed under Platform. Any entry whose home page is currently mid-rebuild is omitted from this index until the rebuild lands.

This page is only as honest as the insights and nuggets the team mines. Internal lore that has not yet been written down — kicked-around findings, partner observations from the field, the unpublished failure modes — is the rate-limiter on this page, not the format. We will keep mining.
