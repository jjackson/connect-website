§ 04.2 · Three pillars of payment

Performance. Direct. Transparent.

01

Performance-based

Workers are paid per verified service. Every action has a clear, known value: ~$60 per completed KMC case, $0.78–$1.30 per CHC visit (by commodity bundle), per-distribution rates for Readers. Complete a visit, get paid.

02

Direct mobile money

Payments go directly to workers' mobile money accounts. No intermediaries. No skimming. No cash logistics. Workers see earnings in real time through the Connect app.

03

End-to-end transparency

From funder to frontline, every dollar is tracked. Workers see their earnings. LLOs see disbursements. Funders see cost per verified outcome. The same data serves every stakeholder.

§ 04.3 · The alignment argument

Why pay-for-outcomes inverts traditional grant dynamics.

Stakeholder Traditional grant model Connect pay-for-outcomes
Funder Pays on projected activities. Financial risk if work doesn't happen. Cost-per-activity-planned. Pays only after verification. Zero risk for undelivered work. Cost-per-verified-outcome.
LLO Funded on capacity assessment. Incentive to win the next grant. Funded per verified delivery. Incentive to recruit and supervise quality workers.
Worker Paid stipends or salaries. No direct link to quality. Paid per verified service. Direct financial motivation to deliver quality.
