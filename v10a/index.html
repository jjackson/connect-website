How It Works

Four steps. One closed loop.

The platform composes the same four primitives across every program — vaccine campaigns, postnatal visits, reading glasses, parenting support. The shape is the same. The programs change. Each link below is what we actually learned at that step.

Step 01 · Learn

Workers train on their phones, then prove competence.

Self-paced offline training, an in-app assessment gate, then daily AI-coach scenario practice and one supervised observation visit. Five layers, not one test.

88%
Score >70% on first observed visit
Knowledge tests didn't produce competent FLWs — we layered an AI coach. 88% score above 70% on first observed visits. →
Step 02 · Deliver

LLOs run delivery. The Trial Run filters in the field.

Locally-led organizations manage recruitment, supervision, and the relationships that move trust. Dimagi provides the rails, not the people who knock on doors.

880K+
CHC visits in 14 months
We invented the Trial Run because pre-vetting LLOs didn't predict performance — 65% conversion through it. →
Step 03 · Verify

Four independent layers confirm every service.

Biometric identity, GPS + microplanning, photo capture, and algorithmic anomaly detection. A fabricated visit must beat all four — adversarially tested.

AUC 0.91
Adversarial fraud test, three coached rounds
Workers naturally cluster — microplans force them into harder areas, and fraud detection hits AUC 0.91. →
Step 04 · Pay

Mobile money, paid on verification, not promises.

Funds flow directly to workers. Funders see cost per verified outcome. CHC inverted the conventional cost-grows-with-scale curve as the network matured.

$1.70
CHC per verified visit (from $2.20)
In CHC, cost per verified visit fell 22% — Nigeria $1.30 with ORS, outside Nigeria $0.78 without. →

Programs running on Connect

Ten programs. One platform.

Each program is a deployment of the LDVP cycle against a specific intervention. Below: the four with dedicated case studies. See the full catalog →

Child Health Campaign

CHC

Door-to-door vitamin A, deworming, ORS, malnutrition screening. 1M+ verified visits across eight countries.

In CHC, microplans tripled visits-per-child from 0.4 to 1.4 and pushed coverage from 84% to 94%. →
Kangaroo Mother Care

KMC

Post-discharge home visits for small and vulnerable newborns. 4,005 cases enrolled (March 2026).

KMC reduces neonatal mortality up to 40%, yet global coverage is below 5% — we close the post-discharge gap. →

Caregivers' ECD attitudes were already correct — we moved knowledge +33% and observed teaching behavior +21%. →

Who Connect serves

Three stakeholders. One aligned chain.

Funders

Every dollar ties to a verified delivery with GPS coordinates, timestamps, and image capture. Cost per verified outcome becomes a real metric, not an estimate. Microplanning ensures coverage reaches the households that get skipped.

Locally-Led Organizations

Sustainable, performance-based contracts with the verification, training, and payment infrastructure provided. Scale up or down against actual demand. Use the same data as proof for your next grant application.

Frontline Workers

Learn new skills digitally on your phone. Deliver meaningful services in your own community. Get paid directly on mobile money when your work is verified. No intermediaries, no delayed payments.
