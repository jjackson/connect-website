# Verify — Page Content

The third step in the LDVP flow. Every service is confirmed before payment.

## Core Message
**Every service confirmed. Not tracked. Not monitored. Verified.** Verification isn't a separate step bolted on after delivery. It's how the system works. Every dollar a funder invests is tied to a verified result — by biometrics, GPS, algorithmic analysis, and geographic coverage models.

## The Three Layers of Verification

### 1. Biometric & Identity Verification
Unique identification confirms that real individuals received real services. No duplicates. No ghosts. No fraud. The PersonalID system prevents FLWs from fabricating beneficiary records or submitting the same delivery multiple times under different names.

### 2. GPS & Geographic Coverage
Geolocation confirms services were delivered in the right places. Microplan execution goes further: it ensures **door-to-door coverage, not convenience sampling**. Workers must close every Delivery Unit in their current area before they can move on.

**Results from the Founders Pledge CHC grant:**
- Microplanned campaigns: **94% of estimated population in visited cells**
- Non-microplanned (experienced LLO): 84%
- Non-Connect benchmark: 82%

The populations missed by non-microplanned campaigns are typically those with lower baseline service coverage — exactly the populations funders want to reach.

### 3. Algorithmic Fraud Detection
AI-powered algorithms analyze patterns across submissions to detect anomalies, flag suspicious activity, and ensure data integrity at scale.

**Adversarial testing results:** In a controlled study, experienced FLWs were paid to submit batches of 100 fake visit records each, with bonuses for the most convincing fakers. Using only three data elements (age, gender, MUAC measurements), a Random Forest model achieved an **AUC of 0.91 ± 0.09** — excellent discrimination. Only **2.5% of real workers** received fraud risk scores higher than even the lowest-scoring fabricator.

This is statistical detection alone — GPS and photo verification add additional layers on top.

## Why Verification Matters
Traditional development programs rely on self-reported data and periodic audits. By the time an audit finds a problem, the money has been spent and the window for course-correction has closed.

Connect builds verification directly into the delivery workflow — it's not a separate step, it's how the system works. Payment only triggers after verification passes. Fraud carries minimal financial risk for funders because **the payer never pays until verified delivery is confirmed**.

## What Each Stakeholder Sees

### Funders
See exactly where their money went and what it achieved. Every dollar ties to a verified delivery, with GPS coordinates, timestamps, and image capture. **Cost per verified outcome becomes a real metric, not an estimate.**

### LLOs
Have auditable proof of delivery for reporting, compliance, and renewed funding. The same verification data serves as performance evidence for their next grant application.

### Workers
Have transparent records of their verified work — which directly determines their pay. No disputes. No delayed payments while managers "review the paperwork."
