At a glance · The programs running on Connect

Four evidence-backed files. Six more arriving in 2026.

Child Health Campaigns

CHC

Nutrition screening, vaccination, referral. 44 LLOs across Nigeria and Kenya. 880K+ verified visits. The program that informed most of the platform.
Kangaroo Mother Care

KMC

5 structured home visits per SVN. WHO-2022 aligned. Active in India. Live: 50% on the 3-day-post-discharge guardrail, and what we're doing about it.
Early Childhood Development

ECD

Malawi pilot. Attitudes at ceiling. Knowledge +33-41%, observed behavior +21%. Expanding to 8-10 visits across 12 months.
Reading Glasses

Readers

Presbyopia screening with Restoring Vision. Trial Run model applied. The open test of whether the Connect architecture generalizes.
