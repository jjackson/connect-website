§ 02.1 · Mechanism

How Connect inverts the delivery model.

01

Guided workflows

CommCare guides FLWs step-by-step through each visit — from registration through counseling, screening, and treatment. The app captures GPS, timestamps, and images for verification. No paper forms. No end-of-day data entry.

02

Locally-led implementation

LLOs manage government approvals, smartphone procurement, localization, and FLW recruitment. They know the community. Connect provides the platform; they provide the relationships.

03

Performance-based, not headcount-based

LLOs are paid per verified service delivered — not per FLW on staff. This inverts the traditional development model. 44 LLOs across 11 countries, with minimal growth in Dimagi's team.

§ 02.3 · Microplanning

A grid overlay that enforces coverage.

CHC innovation · Founders Pledge 2026

Before each campaign, Connect overlays a grid onto the operational area using open-source satellite data (Google Open Buildings, OpenStreetMap, Microsoft). Grid cells with buildings become Delivery Units. DUs group into Service Areas sized for 1–2 weeks of work per FLW.

Workers must close all DUs in their current Service Area before unlocking the next one. This pushes them into areas they'd otherwise skip — the same areas with the lowest baseline service coverage.

Thoroughness under microplanning

1.4 visits per estimated child in microplanned cells — versus 0.4 in Trial Runs without geographic saturation requirements.
