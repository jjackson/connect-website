The platform

Four steps. One cycle.

LDVP · Learn → Deliver → Verify → Pay

The architecture is sequential and composable. A frontline worker cannot deliver without passing Learn. A delivery is not a delivery until Verify confirms it. Payment only flows against a verified record. Each step is a check on the next.

01 / LEARN Competence before compensation FLWs complete digital training and must pass an in-app assessment before they are cleared to deliver. 88% score above 70% on their first observed visits. Read 02 / DELIVER Locally-led, mobile-guided Trained FLWs deliver services guided step-by-step by the Connect app. LLOs handle community relationships; the platform handles everything else. Read 03 / VERIFY Every service confirmed. Not tracked. Verified. Biometric, GPS, photo and algorithmic layers validate every submission. Adversarial tested: AUC 0.91 detecting fabrication from three data elements alone. Read 04 / PAY Mobile money, direct to worker Payment flows only after verification. $90,000+ paid directly to frontline workers to date. No intermediaries, no reconciliation delays. Read

Three audiences. One mechanism.

What you get from Connect depends on where you stand.

Funders

Outcomes, not activities.

Every dollar ties to a verified delivery with GPS, timestamp, and image. Microplanning forces equitable coverage — 94% population reach vs 82% without. Cost per verified outcome drops as the network scales.

Locally-led organizations

Funding without headcount bloat.

Performance-based contracts mean costs track actual delivery, not organizational size. The platform handles verification, payment, and oversight. You handle the relationships we can't centralize.

Frontline workers

Paid directly, paid fairly.

Learn new skills on your phone. Deliver services in your community. Get paid by mobile money for verified work — no intermediaries, no delays. FLWs report substantial income increases.
