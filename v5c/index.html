How It Works

Learn. Deliver. Verify. Pay.

Connect links funding directly to verified service delivery through a transparent four-step model.

1
Learn Digital training via mobile

Frontline workers complete structured digital training modules on their phones, building skills before they enter the field.

2
Deliver Service delivery with CommCare

Workers use CommCare-powered mobile applications to deliver structured health and development interventions in their communities.

3
Verify Biometric, GPS, algorithmic checks

Every service delivery is verified through multiple methods — biometric confirmation, GPS location, and algorithmic quality checks — ensuring accountability.

4
Pay Mobile money, transparent compensation

Verified service delivery triggers transparent, timely payments via mobile money directly to the workers who delivered the service.
