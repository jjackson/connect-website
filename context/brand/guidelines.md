# Dimagi Brand Guidelines 2025

Source: Dimagi External Brand Guidelines PDF

## Color Palette

**Dimagi-specific (primary):**
- **Deep Purple** #16006D (PMS 3542) — Primary, for headings
- **Indigo** #3843D0 (PMS 2126) — Connect brand color

**Full palette (shared):**
- **Sky** #8EA1FF (PMS 7451)
- **Mango** #FC5F36 (PMS 1645) — Accent, CTAs
- **Sunset** #E44434 (PMS 172)
- **Marigold** #FEAF31 (PMS 1235)
- **Dimagi Gradient** — Indigo #3843D0 → Mango #FC5F36

**Text:**
- Title text: Deep Purple #16006D
- Body text: Fern Blue #5F6A7D
- Background: White #FFFFFF, Off-white #FAFBFF, Sky-tint #F0F3FF

**Color rule:** 80/20 split between blues and oranges. "Monochromatic + accent" not "rainbow."

## Typography

- **Primary typeface:** Work Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** Extra Light 200, Normal 400, Semi Bold 600
- Use Extra Light 200 for large display numbers

## Connect Logo

- Two elegant bold strokes rising upwards (swoosh) + "Connect" wordmark
- Primary color: Dimagi Indigo #3843D0
- "by dimagi" lockup: plaintext, locked under logo when standing alone
- Variants in assets/: connect-by-dimagi.png, connect-by-dimagi-white.png, connect-logomark.png, connect-logomark-white.png

## Design Elements

1. **Topography / Sense of Place** — topographic lines (concentric shapes), latitude/longitude lines (solid, dotted, curved). Speaks to meeting partners anywhere.
2. **Shapes / Customized Solutions** — stackable geometric shapes. Speaks to modular, customizable platform.

## Icon Style
- Simple forms, fewest possible marks
- Small gaps/breaks in linework where elements intersect
- Deep Purple on light, white on dark
- Min 68x68px, line thickness min 2px

## Imagery
- Real, authentic photography — sense of place
- Mix color and B&W for editorial feel
- B&W works well for FLW portraits
- Must have subject approval; culturally appropriate
