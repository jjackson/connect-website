§ ECD.1 · The challenge

90% of brain development happens by age 5.

In low- and middle-income countries, 250 million children are at risk of not achieving their developmental potential. Evidence shows nurturing care is critical in the first 1,000 days. Parent-focused interventions have proven positive effects — but caregivers have limited access to evidence-based parenting guidance.

Connect ECD delivers that guidance through structured home visits: caregiver registration, ECD counseling, responsive-caregiving support, talk-and-play for brain development, age-appropriate activities, and assessment of caregiver-child interactions.

§ ECD.4 · AI coach bot

Daily reinforcement, built on Open Chat Studio.

Daily
Coach-Bot interaction

Scenario practice, motivational interviewing, self-reflection.

FLWs interact with the AI coach bot daily (paid for interaction). The bot offers scenario prompts to identify knowledge gaps, practices motivational interviewing techniques (open-ended questions, affirmations, reflective listening, summarizing), and provides tailored feedback based on actual visit experiences.

§ ECD.6 · Research questions

The three-part theory of change.

Q1

Can FLWs deliver?

Can FLWs effectively deliver a new intervention through digital-only training, validated by observed practical tests? Answer: yes — 85% progress, 88% score ≥70%.

Q2

Do caregiver skills change?

Can FLW-delivered behavior change improve responsive caregiving? Answer: yes — +41% knowledge, +21% teaching behavior observed.

Q3

Do child outcomes improve?

Can FLW-delivered ECD improve cognitive, language, motor, and socioemotional child development? Under investigation · expansion phase.
