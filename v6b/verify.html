§ 03.2 · Algorithmic fraud detection

Adversarial testing of the verification algorithm.

Source: Founders Pledge CHC Report · Feb 2026
0.91± 0.09
AUC · Random Forest

Statistical detection alone — before GPS or photo verification adds more layers.

In a controlled study, experienced FLWs were paid to submit batches of 100 fabricated visit records each, with bonuses for the most convincing fakers. Using only three data elements — age, gender, and MUAC measurements — a Random Forest model achieved excellent discrimination between real and fabricated submissions.

Only 2.5% of real workers received fraud risk scores higher than even the lowest-scoring fabricator. This is statistical detection alone. GPS and photo verification add additional layers on top.

§ 03.3 · Three layers

How verification composes.

Layer 01

Biometric & identity

Unique identification confirms that real individuals received real services. No duplicates. No ghosts. The PersonalID system prevents FLWs from fabricating beneficiary records or submitting the same delivery multiple times under different names.

Layer 02

GPS & geographic coverage

Geolocation confirms services were delivered in the right places. Microplan execution goes further: door-to-door coverage, not convenience sampling. Workers must close every Delivery Unit in their current Service Area before they move on.

Layer 03

Algorithmic detection

AI-powered algorithms analyze patterns across submissions to detect anomalies, flag suspicious activity, and ensure data integrity at scale. AUC 0.91 on three data elements alone — before layering GPS and photo signal.

§ 03.4 · What each stakeholder sees

Same verification data. Three different views.

Funders

Cost per verified outcome.

See exactly where the money went and what it achieved. Every dollar ties to a verified delivery, with GPS coordinates, timestamps, and image capture. Cost per verified outcome becomes a real metric, not an estimate.

LLOs

Auditable proof of delivery.

The same verification data serves as evidence for reporting, compliance, and next-grant applications. No more reconstructing delivery records from paper forms at quarter-end.

Workers

Transparent earnings record.

Your verified work record is always visible. No disputes. No delayed payments while managers "review the paperwork." Mobile money lands when verification passes.

$0

Fraud carries minimal financial risk for funders because the payer never pays until verified delivery is confirmed. Traditional programs discover fraud in audit; Connect blocks payment at the source.
