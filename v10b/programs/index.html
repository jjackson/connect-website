Programs with field dispatches

CHC · 8 countries · 44 LLOs · GiveWell, Founders Pledge, Gavi

Child Health Campaign

The richest evidence base in the catalog. The Trial Run, microplanning, adversarial fraud testing, and the cost-inversion curve were all measured here first.

880,000+ verified visits in 14 months · 22% cost reduction · AUC 0.91 fraud detection

KMC · 6 LLO partners · WHO-aligned · neonatal MNCH

Kangaroo Mother Care

Up to 40% reduction in neonatal mortality, yet global coverage is below 5%. Connect KMC closes the post-discharge gap with 4–5 home visits in the first 60 days.

4,005 cases (March 2026) · 12.8 day average enrollment age · 50% within-3-day target — gap published

ECD · Malawi pilot · 76 FLWs · 9,510+ visits

Early Childhood Development

Caregiver attitudes were already correct at baseline. Knowledge of rapid-development timing rose +33pts; observed teaching behavior +21pts. Dosage doubled to 8–10 visits.

27,000+ visits · +33pts knowledge · 47% encouragement-of-autonomy at endline (the hardest sub-domain)
