How It Works / Step 01
Step 01 of 04

Learn

Frontline Workers build skills at their own pace through mobile-first digital training.

01
Feature 01

Mobile-First Training

Workers access training modules on their phones. Interactive content and assessments build health service delivery skills, reaching workers wherever they are without requiring classroom time or travel.

02
Feature 02

Self-Paced Progress

Pre-tests and post-tests measure knowledge. Workers move at their own speed, proving readiness before they begin delivering. Only those who demonstrate competence proceed to service delivery.

03
Feature 03

Locally Relevant Content

Materials localized for each intervention and region. Cultural relevance and practical applicability ensured through collaboration with locally-led organizations who understand their communities.
