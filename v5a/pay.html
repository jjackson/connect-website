Pay

Every Frontline Worker is paid for their impactful work. Fair, transparent compensation tied directly to verified results.

How Payment Works

Fair Pay for Verified Impact

Performance-Based

Workers are paid per verified service delivered. Complete a KMC visit? Get paid. Screen a child for malnutrition? Get paid. Every verified action has a clear, known value.

Mobile Money

Payments go directly to workers' mobile money accounts. No intermediaries, no delays, no skimming. Workers see exactly what they've earned in real-time.

End-to-End Transparency

From funder to frontline, every dollar is tracked. Workers see their earnings. LLOs see disbursements. Funders see cost-per-outcome.

The Model

Aligned Incentives Across the Chain

Connect's pay-for-outcomes model aligns incentives across the entire chain. Workers are motivated to deliver quality services because their pay depends on verification. Funders get accountability because every dollar is tied to a result. LLOs get sustainable funding because they can demonstrate impact.
