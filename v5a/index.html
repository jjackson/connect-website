How It Works

Four Steps From Funding to Verified Impact

Connect's Learn-Deliver-Verify-Pay model creates a transparent, end-to-end pipeline that links every dollar to a verified outcome.

Step 1

Learn

Workers receive digital training through a mobile app, building skills at their own pace with structured modules.

Step 2

Deliver

Workers deliver health services in their communities, using CommCare for structured data collection and case management.

Step 3

Verify

Biometric verification, GPS tracking, and algorithmic validation confirm that services were delivered as reported.

Step 4

Pay

Fair, transparent compensation via mobile money — workers see exactly what they earned and why, building trust and retention.
