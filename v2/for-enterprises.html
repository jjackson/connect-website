Operations

Operate at Scale, Verify at the Individual Level

Managing large frontline programs means balancing scale with quality. CommCare Connect gives you both: a platform that handles training, deployment, verification, and payment across your entire workforce — while verifying every individual service delivery.

Whether you operate in one country or twenty, the system adapts to your operational complexity without sacrificing the granularity you need to maintain standards and demonstrate impact.

Platform

Built for Complex Operations

Integrated Training Pipeline

Onboard and upskill workers through digital training that feeds directly into active service delivery. Workers train on the same platform they use to deliver, creating a seamless pipeline from qualification to deployment.

Workforce Management

Manage assignments, track performance, and ensure quality across distributed teams. Real-time visibility into worker activity and delivery status gives program directors the operational control they need at any scale.

End-to-End Payment

Transparent compensation from verified delivery through payment — no manual reconciliation, no intermediary delays. Workers are paid based on confirmed, verified work, and every transaction is auditable.

Infrastructure

Proven Infrastructure

Built on CommCare, the platform used by 350,000+ frontline workers in 80+ countries for over 15 years. This is not a prototype. It is production infrastructure for the hardest operating environments on earth.

CommCare Connect inherits that reliability and extends it with workforce coordination, verified delivery, and integrated payment — purpose-built for organizations that cannot afford system failures in the field.
