Sustainable Funding

Connected to Sustainable Funding

LLOs are closest to the communities they serve. Connect makes that visible.

Locally-led organizations understand their communities better than anyone. You know the needs, the people, and the context. But too often, that deep knowledge is invisible to the funders who could support your work.

Connect changes that. By verifying every service your workers deliver, Connect creates an auditable record of outcomes that funders can trust. Your impact becomes visible, measurable, and fundable — creating a sustainable path to the resources your organization needs to grow.

What Changes for Your LLO

Access to Sustainable Funding

Verified outcomes create a track record that funders can trust. Connect makes your impact visible, helping you move beyond short-term grants toward sustained, outcomes-based funding that grows with your organization.

FLW Training & Growth

Equip your frontline workers with digital training that builds real skills. Workers learn at their own pace, gain new competencies, and deliver higher-quality services — strengthening your organization from within.

Fair, Timely Payment

Workers see exactly what they have earned and when they will be paid. Transparent compensation managed through the platform means no hidden deductions, no delays, and no guesswork for your team.

Built for the Field

Built for Where LLOs Work

Low Bandwidth

Works where connectivity is limited.

Connect is built on CommCare, a platform designed for low-resource settings. It works on low-bandwidth networks, so your workers can collect data and deliver services even in areas with limited or intermittent connectivity.

Basic Smartphones

Runs on the devices your workers already own.

No expensive hardware required. Connect runs on basic Android smartphones, meeting your workforce where they are. Workers use the devices they already carry to learn, deliver, and get verified.

Offline Sync

Collect data offline. Sync when connected.

Workers can complete forms and record service delivery even without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically when connectivity is restored, so no work is ever lost and your records stay complete.
