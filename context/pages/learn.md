# Learn — Page Content

The first step in the LDVP flow. Workers learn skills before they can deliver.

## Core Message
**Digital training that prepares workers before they deliver.** Before any FLW begins service delivery through Connect, they must complete mandatory digital training and pass an in-app knowledge assessment. No classroom time. No placeholder credentials. Competence verified before compensation.

## Three Features

### 1. Mobile-First Training
Workers access training modules on their own phones via the Learn app. Self-paced. Offline-capable. No classroom scheduling, no travel costs. Content includes interactive lessons, practice scenarios, and assessment-driven progression.

### 2. Locally Relevant Content
Training materials are localized for each intervention and region — ensuring cultural relevance and practical applicability. Content is approved by subject matter experts (often the LLO or a program-specific SME identified by Dimagi per geography).

### 3. Assessment-Gated Progression
FLWs must pass an in-app learning assessment before they're cleared to deliver services. If they fail, they can retake the module and try again. The assessment is standard across all LLOs and FLWs for a given program — the bar for competence is the same everywhere.

## What Workers Learn (examples by program)
- **KMC** — KMC positioning, danger sign recognition, exclusive breastfeeding counseling
- **CHC** — Vitamin A dosing, deworming eligibility, MUAC screening technique, vaccine hesitancy navigation
- **ECD** — Responsive caregiving, motivational interviewing, age-appropriate activities
- **Readers** — Near vision screening, lens fitting (+1.0 to +3.0 diopters)

## Evidence It Works

### ECD Pilot Validation (Malawi)
- **85% of FLWs** successfully progressed through digital learning to service delivery
- **88% of FLWs scored above 70%** on their first observed field visits
- 77% pass rate on supervised observation visits
- Validated through iterative experiments with ~400 FLWs
- (Source: ECD Feb 2026 report)

### CLP Self-Service Validation
Locally-led organizations rated the digital training resources **9-9.5 out of 10** when operating without direct Dimagi support. All 7 Stage 2 organizations completed vaccine promotion campaigns using only digital resources.
- (Source: CLP Stage 2 Report, Feb 2026)

### AI Coach (ECD program)
Built on Dimagi's Open Chat Studio platform. FLWs interact with the AI coach bot **daily**:
- Offers example scenarios to identify knowledge gaps
- Practices motivational interviewing techniques
- Provides tailored feedback based on self-reported visit experiences
- Reinforces learning beyond the initial module

## The Connect Advantage
Traditional training requires classroom time, travel costs, and ongoing expert supervision — all of which scale linearly with workers. Connect's digital training model scales asymmetrically: adding the 10,000th FLW costs almost nothing. And because progression depends on passing assessments, **quality isn't sacrificed for scale**.
