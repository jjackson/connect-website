Three Pillars of Verification

How Every Service Gets Confirmed

Biometric Verification

Unique identification confirms that real individuals received real services. No duplicates, no ghosts, no fraud.

GPS & Location Tracking

Geolocation confirms services were delivered in the right places. Microplan execution ensures door-to-door coverage, not convenience sampling.

Algorithmic Validation

AI-powered algorithms analyze patterns across submissions to detect anomalies, flag suspicious activity, and ensure data integrity at scale.

Why Verification Matters

Built In, Not Bolted On

Traditional development programs rely on self-reported data and periodic audits. Connect builds verification directly into the delivery workflow — it's not a separate step, it's how the system works. Every dollar a funder invests is tied to a verified result.
