Ten active programs. One platform underneath.

Connect is not a single intervention. It is the mechanism — Learn, Deliver, Verify, Pay — applied to a growing list of proven services. Four programs have their own detail pages with the learnings behind them; the rest are in the catalog as "coming soon" or early-stage.

Child Health & Nutrition

Child Health Campaign

Door-to-door Vitamin A, deworming, ORS, MUAC screening, and immunization promotion for children 0–59 months. The richest program on the platform and the source of most of our learnings about verification at scale.

Nigeria, DRC, CAR, Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Zambia, Sierra LeoneLive
Maternal & Newborn Health

Kangaroo Mother Care

Post-discharge home visits for small and vulnerable newborns. Aligned with WHO 2022 KMC guidance. Closing the gap between hospital care and the first 28 days of life at home.

Uganda, Kenya, India, NigeriaLive
Health / Parenting

Early Childhood Development

A parenting intervention delivered through structured home visits. Expanding from 3 visits to 8–10 over 12 months, based on pilot evidence about where behavior change actually moves.

Malawi, Mozambique, NigeriaLive
Health / Livelihood

Readers

15,000 pairs of reading glasses to adults with presbyopia in Northeast Nigeria, in partnership with RestoringVision. A test of the Connect model in a new, non-child-health domain.

Nigeria · Restoring VisionLaunching
