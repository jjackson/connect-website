# Deliver — Page Content

The second step in the LDVP flow. Trained FLWs deliver proven services.

## Core Message
**Proven health services delivered through locally-led organizations, guided by mobile.** Once workers pass training, they begin delivering services in their communities using a mobile app that guides every interaction — registration, counseling, screening, treatment, distribution. LLOs manage the local operations; the platform handles the rest.

## Three Features

### 1. Guided Workflows
CommCare guides FLWs step-by-step through each visit — from registration through counseling, screening, and treatment delivery. The app captures GPS, timestamps, and images for verification. No paper forms. No end-of-day data entry. Every interaction is structured and captured as it happens.

### 2. Locally-Led Implementation
LLOs — local nonprofits, community health organizations, and social enterprises — manage government approvals, smartphone procurement, localization, and FLW recruitment and onboarding. They know the community. Connect provides the platform; they provide the relationships.

### 3. Performance-Based, Not Headcount-Based
LLOs are paid per verified service delivered — not per FLW on staff. This inverts the traditional development model. Connect has scaled to 44 LLOs across 11 countries with minimal growth in Dimagi's team, because costs track delivery rather than organizational size.

## Service Types Currently Delivered
- **Door-to-door household visits** — CHC, KMC, ECD, Readers
- **Community health campaigns** — Vitamin A, deworming, immunization promotion
- **Screening and assessment** — Vision, malnutrition, danger signs
- **Counseling and education** — Breastfeeding, KMC, ECD parenting guidance, vaccine hesitancy
- **Product distribution** — Reading glasses, ORS/Zinc packs, KMC wraps, therapeutic food

## Scale (as of Dec 2025/early 2026)
- **1,000,000+ verified services delivered**

- **8+ countries** in active delivery

## How LLO Activation Works (from Founders Pledge learnings)
1. **EOI call** — Simple Expression of Interest: brief scope doc + Google Form, circulated via Dimagi's professional network. A single EOI yielded ~100 applications.
2. **Trial Run** — New LLOs get a small initial contract ($3K setup for 3,000 verified visits). Evaluated on actual delivery speed and quality.
3. **Scale-up** — LLOs that pass the Trial Run receive larger campaigns. Performance consistently accelerates with experience.

**Results:** Of 37 LLOs contracted via Founders Pledge grant:
- 24 (65%) entered Trial Runs and were offered larger campaigns
- 10 underperformed and weren't continued
- 3 went straight to larger campaigns (prior track record)
- 17 completed campaigns following successful trials

## Geographic Equity: Microplanning (CHC Innovation)
Before each campaign, Connect overlays a grid onto the operational area using open-source satellite data (Google Open Buildings, OpenStreetMap, Microsoft). Grid cells with buildings become **Delivery Units (DUs)**. Workers must close all DUs in their current Service Area before unlocking the next — **pushing them into areas they'd otherwise skip**.

**Coverage results:**
- Microplanned campaigns: 94% of estimated population reached
- Without microplans (experienced LLO): 84%
- Well-run non-Connect CommCare campaign: 82%

**Thoroughness:** 1.4 visits per estimated child in microplanned cells, vs 0.4 in Trial Runs without geographic saturation requirements.

## AI-Powered Supervision (Kenya pilot)
Conversational AI chatbot on Dimagi's Open Chat Studio platform. FLWs ask questions via WhatsApp — dosing, eligibility, procedures — and get immediate, contextually appropriate guidance.
- **500 of 511 queries (97.8%) handled without human escalation**
- Average response time: 5 seconds
- Independent evaluation: 88.7% accuracy, 81.5% usefulness
