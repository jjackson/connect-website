Who Connect serves

Three stakeholders. One aligned chain.

i.

Funders

Every dollar ties to a verified delivery with GPS coordinates, timestamps, and image capture. Cost per verified outcome becomes a real metric, not an estimate. Microplanning ensures coverage reaches the households that get skipped — coverage rose from 82–84% to 94% in the cells where the grid ran.

ii.

Locally-Led Organizations

Sustainable, performance-based contracts with the verification, training, and payment infrastructure provided. Scale up or down against actual demand. The Trial Run de-risks activation — $3,000 setup for 3,000 verified visits, then a larger contract if performance holds.

iii.

Frontline Workers

Learn new skills digitally on your phone. Deliver meaningful services in your own community. Get paid directly on mobile money when your work is verified — $90,000+ disbursed against verified visits, no intermediaries, no delayed payments.
