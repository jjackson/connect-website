How It Works

Four steps. One closed loop.

The platform composes the same four primitives across every program — vaccine campaigns, postnatal visits, reading glasses, parenting support. The shape is the same. The programs change.

Step 01 · Learn

Workers train on their phones, then pass a test.

Self-paced, offline-capable digital training. An in-app assessment gates access to service delivery. No paper credentials.

88%
Score >70% on first observed visit
See the training model
Step 02 · Deliver

LLOs run delivery. The app guides every visit.

Locally-led organizations manage recruitment and supervision. CommCare guides each interaction; GPS and timestamps capture as it happens.

880K+
CHC visits in 14 months
How delivery scales
Step 03 · Verify

Four independent layers confirm every service.

Biometric identity, GPS, image capture, and algorithmic anomaly detection. Verification isn't a checkpoint — it's how delivery is defined.

AUC 0.91

Mobile money, paid on verification, not promises.

Funds flow directly to workers. Funders see cost per verified outcome. Cost per visit has fallen 22% as the network has grown.

Who Connect serves

Three stakeholders. One aligned chain.

Funders

Every dollar ties to a verified delivery with GPS coordinates, timestamps, and image capture. Cost per verified outcome becomes a real metric, not an estimate. Microplanning ensures coverage reaches the households that get skipped.

Locally-Led Organizations

Sustainable, performance-based contracts with the verification, training, and payment infrastructure provided. Scale up or down against actual demand. Use the same data as proof for your next grant application.

Frontline Workers

Learn new skills digitally on your phone. Deliver meaningful services in your own community. Get paid directly on mobile money when your work is verified. No intermediaries, no delayed payments.
