How Verification Works

Three Layers of Verification

Biometric Verification

Fingerprint and face confirmation ensure the right worker delivered to the right beneficiary. Every interaction is tied to a verified identity, removing ambiguity from the data.

GPS Tracking

Location data confirms services were delivered where they should be. Geographic verification adds a layer of accountability that self-reported data simply cannot match.

Algorithmic Validation

Data patterns are analyzed to flag anomalies and confirm legitimate delivery. If something looks off, the system catches it before payments are processed.

By Design

Trust Built Into the Platform

Verification is not an afterthought on CommCare Connect. It is not a separate module bolted onto existing workflows. It is woven into every step, from the moment a worker accepts a task to the moment a funder sees the outcome.

This is what separates CommCare Connect from platforms that rely on self-reported impact data. When a service is marked as delivered, it has been confirmed through biometrics, validated by location, and checked against data patterns. Funders do not have to take anyone's word for it. The verification speaks for itself.

The result is a level of trust that makes performance-based funding possible. When every delivery is verified, every dollar can be accounted for, and every worker can be paid fairly for the work they actually did.
