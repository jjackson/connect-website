How delivery works

Three mechanisms that make delivery scale without breaking.

Guided workflows

CommCare walks FLWs step-by-step through each visit — registration, counseling, screening, treatment. The app captures GPS, timestamps, and images as they happen. No paper forms. No end-of-day data entry.

Locally-led implementation

LLOs — local nonprofits, community health organizations, social enterprises — handle government approvals, smartphone procurement, localization, FLW recruitment and onboarding. They know the community. Connect provides the platform.

Performance-based, not headcount-based

LLOs are paid per verified service delivered — not per FLW on staff. Connect has scaled to 44 LLOs across 11 countries with minimal growth in Dimagi's team, because costs track delivery instead of organizational size.

LLO activation model

From EOI to retention — a funnel validated by 44 partners.

Drawn from the Founders Pledge grant period. A single EOI yielded ~100 applications. The model:

EOI Call

Brief scope doc + Google Form, circulated via Dimagi's professional network. Lightweight, fast, and surfaces the best-fit organizations.

~100 applications

Trial Run

Small initial contract — typically $3K setup for 3,000 verified visits. Evaluates each LLO on actual delivery speed and quality, not projections.

37 contracted

Scale-up

LLOs that pass the Trial Run receive larger campaigns. Performance consistently accelerates with experience.

24 (65%) promoted

Retention

17 LLOs completed additional campaigns following successful trials. 10 underperformed and weren't continued. 3 jumped straight to larger campaigns on prior track record.

17 retained

Source: Founders Pledge Final Report, Feb 2026

AI-powered supervision

Support that scales with the workforce.

A conversational AI on Dimagi's Open Chat Studio. FLWs ask questions via WhatsApp — dosing, eligibility, procedures — and get immediate, contextually-appropriate guidance.

Kenya CHC Pilot
500 of 511 queries handled without human escalation.

That's 97.8% self-resolved, with an average response time of 5 seconds. Independent evaluation measured 88.7% accuracy and 81.5% usefulness. The AI supervisor operates 24/7, so FLWs don't wait for office hours to get answers.

Implementation note
Built on Open Chat Studio — Dimagi's own LLM platform.

The same platform powers the ECD coach, CHC supervisor, and future AI agents for new programs. Built on OCS means Connect isn't dependent on a single vendor. Guardrails, logging, and evaluation are first-class.
