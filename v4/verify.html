How It Works

Verify.

Not self-reported. Not estimated. Every service confirmed through biometrics, GPS, and algorithmic validation. Every dollar tied to a result.

Verification

Three Layers of Verification

Biometric Verification

Fingerprint and identity confirmation ensure that each service reaches a real, unique beneficiary. No duplication, no ghost recipients -- every interaction is tied to an individual.

GPS Tracking

Location data confirms that services are delivered where they are needed most. Every delivery is geo-stamped to validate that workers were on-site at the point of care.

Algorithmic Validation

Machine learning models analyze patterns across submissions, flagging anomalies and ensuring data integrity. Cross-referencing timing, location, and biometric data catches inconsistencies humans would miss.

Built-In Trust

Trust Built Into the Platform

Verification is not an afterthought or an add-on. It is woven into every workflow -- from the moment a worker begins a service delivery to the instant payment is processed.

Every data point is captured automatically as part of the natural service delivery process. Workers do not fill out additional forms or perform extra steps. Verification happens seamlessly, reducing friction while increasing confidence.

Funders invest in verified outcomes, not promises. Every dollar is traceable to a confirmed result, making Connect the accountability layer that global development has been missing.
