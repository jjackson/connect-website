Active · Evidence-backed files

Four programs with their own files.

Child Health Campaigns

CHC

Nutrition screening (MUAC), vaccination, treatment referral. 44 LLOs across Nigeria and Kenya. 880K+ verified visits in 14 months. The program where most of Connect's insights were first measured — microplanning, adversarial fraud testing, Trial Run, cost inversion, and the CAR failure we published.
Kangaroo Mother Care

KMC

Five structured home visits per SVN. Aligned to the WHO 2022 guideline update. Active in India. Live: 50% on the 3-day-post-discharge guardrail against a 70% target, and what we're doing about it.
Early Childhood Development

ECD

Responsive caregiving, talk-and-play, behavioral rehearsal. Malawi pilot moved knowledge +33-41% and observed behavior +21%. Expanding from 3 visits to 8-10 across 12 months.
Reading Glasses

Readers

Presbyopia screening and distribution with Restoring Vision. The first non-child-health sector on Connect. An open test of whether the architecture generalizes.
