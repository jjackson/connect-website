Part IV · Who it's for

Three stakeholders. One verified ledger.

For Funders

Invest in verified outcomes, not activity plans.

Every dollar ties to a delivered, verified service. Microplanning drives equitable reach. Cost-per-verified-outcome is a real metric — and it dropped 22% as the platform scaled.

For LLOs

Sustainable funding, no overhead inflation.

Performance-based contracts let you scale without adding central-team overhead. Connect handles verification, payment, oversight. You keep the community relationships.

For FLWs

Learn digitally. Earn directly.

Training on your own phone. Visits guided in-app. Mobile money paid to your account on verification. No intermediaries, no paper, no delays.

Close the loop. Put funding in reach of the frontline.
