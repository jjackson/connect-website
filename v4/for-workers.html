Your Work

Your Work, Your Choice

Connect puts you in control of your work.

You choose the tasks you want to take on. You see exactly what you will earn before you start. You decide when and where you work. There are no hidden deductions, no surprises, and no middlemen taking a cut.

Every task you complete is recorded and verified. Your earnings are tracked transparently, so you always know what you are owed. Connect respects your time, your skills, and the essential work you do in your community.
