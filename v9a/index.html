Connect is a new model for global development. Funding flows only when service delivery is verified at the frontline — by biometric, GPS, and algorithmic layers working together. A growing network of 44 locally-led organisations has delivered over one million visits under this model in 14 months.

The Cycle

Four steps. One closed loop.

Every step is instrumented. Workers must pass training before they deliver. Every service is verified before it is paid. The sequence is infrastructure — repeatable across programs, countries, and partners.

Digital training with a pass bar.

Self-paced, offline-capable modules. FLWs must pass an in-app assessment before they can deliver.

Who it's for

Three stakeholders. One verified ledger.

Funders

Invest in verified outcomes, not activity plans.

Every dollar ties to a delivered service. Microplanning pushes coverage into the communities that would otherwise be missed. Cost-per-verified-outcome is a real metric — and it dropped 22% as the platform scaled.

Locally-Led Orgs

Sustainable funding without adding overhead.

Performance-based contracts let you scale delivery without inflating a central team. The platform handles verification, payment and oversight. You keep the community relationships — that's the L in LLO.

Frontline Workers

Learn digitally. Earn directly.

Training on your own phone. Visits guided step-by-step in-app. Mobile money paid to your account on verification. No intermediaries, no paper, no delayed payments.
