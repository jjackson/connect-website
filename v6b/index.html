§ 04 · Three Audiences

One platform. Three sets of incentives, aligned.

For Funders

Verified outcomes, not estimates.

Every dollar ties to a delivered service — with GPS, timestamps, image capture, and biometric confirmation. Cost per verified outcome becomes a real metric. Microplanning ensures equitable reach. Cost drops as the network scales.

For LLOs

Sustainable funding. Scale without overhead.

Performance-based contracts let you grow impact without growing administrative weight. The platform handles verification, payment, and oversight infrastructure — you bring the local relationships and delivery capacity.

For Workers

Learn. Deliver. Get paid — directly.

Mobile money sent to your account the moment your visit verifies. No intermediaries. No delays. Your earnings and verified work record are always visible in the Connect app.
