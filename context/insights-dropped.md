# Dropped Insights — v8 Audit (2026-04-14)

No insights were dropped in the v0.2.36 scoping audit. All 10 survived — but 6 of 10 required rescoping from an implicit "Platform" framing to a program-specific scope:

- **Insight 2** (microplanning numbers) rescoped from Platform → **CHC**. Mechanism remains platform-portable; the 0.4/1.4 visits/child and 82-84% → 94% coverage figures are CHC-measured and are named as CHC on every page that uses them.
- **Insight 4** (22% cost reduction, $2.20 → $1.70) rescoped from implicit Platform → **CHC**. This was the biggest single fix in the audit: prior site versions (v7) presented "Connect got leaner — 22% reduction" as a Connect-wide platform claim. It is not — those are CHC cost-per-verified-visit numbers from the Founders Pledge grant period. The *structural reasons* (LLO economics, performance-based payment, integrated tooling) remain platform-level; the **number** is named to CHC everywhere it appears.
- **Insight 5** (no displacement) rescoped to **CHC** — Gombe/Sokoto survey was inside the CHC program in Nigeria. Cultural generalization flagged but not claimed.
- **Insight 6** (religious leaders) rescoped to **CHC** — CLP Stage 2 vaccine campaign is CHC.
- **Insight 9** (CAR failure) rescoped to **CHC** — the contracting failure was CHC vaccine work. Lesson is portable; incident is CHC.
- **Insight 10** (guardrails philosophy) kept as Platform (it's a configuration framework every program inherits) but the live example (50% vs 70% on 3-day-post-discharge) is now named to **KMC** explicitly.
