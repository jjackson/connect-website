Service Delivery

Services at the Last Mile

Frontline workers use CommCare to guide service delivery in the field. The platform handles data collection, workflow management, and case tracking, giving workers the tools they need to deliver quality care in even the most remote settings.

  • Structured workflows guide workers through each visit, ensuring consistency and quality across every interaction.
  • Case tracking follows individuals over time, building a complete picture of care delivered.
  • Works offline and syncs automatically when connectivity returns, so service delivery never stops.
Data Infrastructure

Every Interaction Captured

Delivery data flows from the field to the platform in real time. From a home visit in rural Cameroon to a vaccination in Dhaka, every service interaction generates verified, structured data that funders and program managers can trust.

  • Real-time data capture ensures that delivery information is available the moment it happens, not weeks later in a spreadsheet.
  • Offline-first design means workers never lose data, even in areas with limited or no connectivity.
  • Structured workflows ensure quality by guiding workers through standardized processes, reducing errors and variability.
  • Proven infrastructure across 80+ countries and 350,000+ frontline workers demonstrates that this approach works at scale.
