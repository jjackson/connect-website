Step 2

Deliver

Frontline workers deliver proven health services through trusted, locally-led organizations using CommCare for data collection and workflow management.

How Delivery Works

Structured, Guided Service Delivery

Guided Workflows

CommCare guides FLWs step-by-step through each service visit — from registration through counseling, screening, and treatment delivery.

Locally-Led Implementation

LLOs manage government approvals, procure smartphones, localize content, and recruit and onboard FLWs in their communities.

Real-Time Data

Every interaction is captured digitally, giving program managers real-time visibility into service delivery across all regions.

Service Types

What Gets Delivered

  • Door-to-door household visits (CHC, KMC, ECD, Readers)
  • Community health campaigns (Vitamin A, deworming, immunization)
  • Screening and assessment (vision, malnutrition, danger signs)
  • Counseling and education (breastfeeding, KMC, ECD)
  • Product distribution (reading glasses, ORS, therapeutic food)
