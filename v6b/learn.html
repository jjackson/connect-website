§ 01.1 · Mechanism

Three design choices make training scale without classroom.

Source: Connect Platform Architecture
01

Mobile-first training

Workers access training modules on their own phones via the Learn app. Self-paced. Offline-capable. No classroom scheduling, no travel costs. Content includes interactive lessons, practice scenarios, and assessment-driven progression.

02

Locally relevant content

Training is localized for each intervention and region — ensuring cultural relevance and practical applicability. Content is approved by subject-matter experts identified by Dimagi per geography.

03

Assessment-gated progression

FLWs must pass an in-app learning assessment before they're cleared to deliver services. If they fail, they can retake the module. The bar for competence is the same across every LLO and every region.

§ 01.2 · Evidence

Validated with ~400 FLWs across the ECD pilot.

Source: ECD Feb 2026 Report · CLP Stage 2

ECD pilot validation · Malawi

85% of FLWs progressed from digital learning into service delivery. 88% scored above 70% on their first observed field visits. Iterative experiments refined the autonomous learning model with ~400 FLWs before scale-up.

ECD Feb 2026 Report · 76 FLWs · 9,510+ completed visits

CLP self-service validation

Locally-led organizations rated digital training resources 9.0–9.5 / 10 when operating without direct Dimagi support. All 7 Stage 2 organizations completed vaccine promotion campaigns using only digital resources.

AI Coach · Query Handling

The AI coach handles the long tail.

FLWs interact daily with an AI-powered coach bot built on Dimagi's Open Chat Studio platform. It offers scenario prompts, practices motivational interviewing, and provides tailored feedback after each visit. In a Kenya pilot, it handled 500 of 511 queries without human escalation. Independent evaluation: 88.7% accuracy, 81.5% usefulness.
