For Local Organizations.

Connect your community health workers to funding, training, and fair compensation — all through one platform.

Partnership

Your Community, Connected to Global Funding

Local organizations are closest to the communities they serve. You understand the context, the needs, and the people doing the work. What has been missing is a clear line between that frontline effort and the funding that sustains it.

CommCare Connect bridges that gap. Every service your workers deliver is captured, verified, and made visible to funders through the platform. Verified delivery data means your impact is no longer invisible — it becomes the foundation for sustainable funding streams that keep your programs running.

The Difference

What Changes for Your Organization

Access to Funding

Verified delivery data makes your impact visible to funders — creating a direct link between the work your team does and the resources that sustain it. No more relying on narrative reports alone. The data speaks for itself.

Worker Training & Growth

Your workers access digital training modules, build new skills, and take on new service delivery opportunities — all through the platform. Growth happens on their terms, at their pace, in their community.

Fair, Timely Payment

Your workers see exactly what they have earned and when they will be paid — transparent compensation that respects their contribution. No hidden deductions, no unexplained delays.

Designed for Reality

Built for Where You Work

CommCare Connect works in low-bandwidth environments, on basic smartphones, and syncs when connectivity returns. The platform is designed for the operating realities of community-based organizations — not Silicon Valley assumptions about infrastructure.

Your workers should not need a strong signal to do their jobs. And your organization should not need enterprise IT to manage a program. CommCare Connect meets you where you are, so you can focus on what matters: serving your community.
