# Connect by Dimagi — Official Messaging

Source: Connect Marketing Hub (Confluence), pulled 2026-04-14

## Tagline
**Connect: Powering the Frontline. Paying for Results.**

## Elevator Pitch
Connect is building a new model for global development—one that directly links funding to verified service delivery at the frontline, enabling more transparency, accountability, and impact.

## 50-Word Description
Connect by Dimagi is a global platform for funding verified service delivery. We enable funders to invest in proven interventions delivered by a growing network of trusted local organizations, ensuring every frontline worker is paid for their impactful work and every dollar is tied to a result.

## 100-Word Description
Connect by Dimagi is building a new model for global development that links funding directly to verified service delivery at the frontline. Our platform connects funders with a network of trusted, locally-led organizations (LLOs) to deliver proven, high-impact services. Frontline Workers (FLWs) use a mobile app to learn skills, deliver services, and verify that their work meets quality standards in order to get paid. For funders, Connect offers a transparent, cost-effective way to invest in verified outcomes. For LLOs, it provides access to sustainable funding and tools to support delivering these vital services.

## Key Terminology (Approved)
- **Connect by Dimagi** — not "CommCare Connect" (CommCare is the underlying platform)
- **Locally-Led Organizations (LLOs)** — not "local organizations"
- **Frontline Workers (FLWs)** — approved term
- **verified outcomes** — the funder value prop
- **pay for outcomes, not effort** — key theme

## Three Audiences
1. **Funders** — transparent, cost-effective way to invest in verified outcomes
2. **LLOs** — access to sustainable funding and tools
3. **FLWs** — learn skills, deliver services, verify work, get paid

## Core Stats (as of Dec 2025/early 2026)
- 1,000,000+ verified services delivered
- 880,000+ CHC visits in 14 months (Founders Pledge)
- 44 LLO partners across 11 countries
- $5.9M in funding raised (vs $2M target)
- $90,000+ in direct FLW payments
- 5,000+ FLWs trained
- 12 implementation countries: Ethiopia, Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Nigeria, Mozambique, Malawi, Zambia, Sierra Leone, DRC, CAR, India
