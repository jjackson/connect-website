Programs · the catalog of Connect deployments

Ten programs. Four with public depth pages. Each with named scope.

Connect is a platform that runs program-shaped workloads. Each program below has its own LDVP cycle, its own LLO cohort, and its own published evidence base. CHC is the richest; KMC and ECD have published pilot data; Readers is in pre-cohort with named partners. Six more are in development. Click into any of the four with depth pages to read the engineering retrospective.

Active programs with public depth

CHC · 8 countries

Child Health Campaign

880,000+ verified visits in 14 months. Microplanning, adversarial fraud testing, the Trial Run model, and the cost-inversion curve were all measured here.

Funders: Founders Pledge · GiveWell · Gavi · 44 LLOs

KMC · 6 LLO partners

Kangaroo Mother Care

Closing the post-discharge gap for small or vulnerable newborns. 4,005 cases as of March 2026; 50% within-3-days enrollment against a 70% target.

Standards: WHO 2022 KMC guidance

ECD · Malawi pilot

Early Childhood Development

Caregivers' attitudes were already correct — knowledge and behavior were the gap. 76 FLWs, 9,510+ visits, 50-caregiver baseline/endline panel.

Cost: $7–$18 per child

Partner: RestoringVision
