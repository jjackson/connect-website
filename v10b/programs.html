Ten programs. One platform.

Each program is a deployment of the LDVP cycle against a specific intervention. Four have dedicated case studies with field data. Six are coming online in 2026.

Child Health Campaign
prog=chc
child_health

Door-to-door vitamin A, deworming, ORS, malnutrition screening, and immunization promotion. 1M+ verified visits across 8 countries; 880K+ in the FP grant window.

n=44 LLOs · 8 countries open
Kangaroo Mother Care
prog=kmc
newborn

Post-discharge home visits for small and vulnerable newborns. Reduces neonatal mortality up to 40%; closes the gap after the hospital door closes. 4,005 cases enrolled by March 2026.

n=6 LLOs · 4 countries open
Early Childhood Development
prog=ecd
child_dev

Parenting intervention validated with ~400 FLWs across Malawi pilot. Knowledge +33%, observed teaching behavior +21%. Doubled visit dosage 3 → 8-10 across 12 months.

n=76 FLWs · 27,000+ visits open
Reading Glasses
prog=readers
vision

Near-vision screening and distribution for presbyopia in adults 35+. Partnership with RestoringVision (15,000 pairs available). Launching in northeast Nigeria.

NE Nigeria · 2026 launch open
