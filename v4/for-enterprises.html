The Challenge

Operate at Scale, Verify at the Individual Level

Scale + Quality

Balancing scale with quality is the hardest problem in frontline operations.

Enterprises need to deploy thousands of workers across vast geographies while ensuring every single interaction meets quality standards. Connect solves this by embedding verification into every step of the delivery process -- biometric confirmation, GPS tracking, and algorithmic validation ensure that scale never comes at the cost of quality. Every worker, every task, every outcome is individually verified.

Capabilities

Built for Complex Operations

Integrated Training

Learn before you deliver

Workers complete structured training modules directly in the Connect app before accessing delivery tasks. Training is tied to certification -- workers only unlock tasks they are qualified to perform, ensuring consistent quality at every level.

Workforce Management

Transparent, end-to-end

Manage thousands of frontline workers with full visibility into performance, availability, and location. Workers choose their own tasks, set their own pace, and see their earnings in real time -- reducing churn and improving motivation across your workforce.

End-to-End Payment

Fair pay, fast delivery

Every completed and verified task triggers automatic payment. No manual processing, no delayed compensation. The entire payment cycle is visible and auditable -- from task completion through verification to payment delivery.

Proven Infrastructure

Built on CommCare -- the platform trusted by 350,000+ frontline workers in 80+ countries for 15+ years. Connect extends CommCare's proven infrastructure with verified delivery, integrated training, and transparent payment to power enterprise-scale operations.
