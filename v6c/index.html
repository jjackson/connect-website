Platform capabilities

Everything verified delivery needs, composed in one place.

Each capability is a building block. Programs like KMC, CHC, ECD, and Readers are just different configurations of the same platform.

Microplanning grids from satellite data

Overlays an open-source geospatial grid (Google Open Buildings, OSM, Microsoft) onto any operational area. Workers close Delivery Units sequentially — pushing them into places they'd otherwise skip. 94% population coverage versus 82% for traditional campaigns.

Assessment-gated training

FLWs must pass a standardized in-app test before delivery. 77% supervised-visit pass rate across the Malawi ECD pilot.

Biometric PersonalID

Unique identification prevents duplicates, ghost visits, and cross-FLW fraud before it's ever recorded.

GPS coverage audit

Every visit stamps location + timestamp. Coverage maps reveal which populations were reached — in real time.

Fraud detection ML

AUC 0.91 from just 3 data elements. Statistically detects fabricated data even without GPS or photos.

AI supervision on WhatsApp

Conversational AI on Open Chat Studio handled 97.8% of FLW queries without human escalation in the Kenya pilot. Average response time: 5 seconds.

Pay-on-verification via mobile money

Payment only triggers after verification passes. $90,000+ paid directly to FLWs. Performance-based contracts invert the traditional grant model.

Who it's for

One platform, three audiences, aligned incentives.

For Funders

Pay for outcomes, not effort.

A transparent, cost-effective way to invest in verified outcomes. Every dollar ties to a delivered service with GPS, timestamp, and image capture. Microplanning ensures equitable coverage. Cost-per-verified-outcome drops as the network scales.

For LLOs

Sustainable funding, modern tools.

Performance-based contracts let you scale without adding overhead. The platform handles verification, payment, and oversight infrastructure so you can focus on the work that only locally-led organizations can do.

For FLWs

Paid for your impact.

Learn new skills digitally on your phone. Deliver meaningful services in your community. Get paid directly via mobile money for verified work — no intermediaries, no delays, no missing paperwork.
