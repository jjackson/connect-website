§5 · Programs · catalog of active deployments

Ten programs running on Connect. Four with public depth.

Connect is a platform; programs are what runs on it. The four cards with detail links — CHC, KMC, ECD, Readers — have published evidence and a working memo behind them. The other six are in pilot or planning. We list them with what we know, not what we hope.

CHC

Child Health Campaign

VAS, deworming, ORS/Zinc co-pack. The richest evidence base in the catalog: 880,000+ verified visits in 14 months, 22% cost reduction, microplanning at 94% coverage.

Nigeria · DRC · CAR · Kenya · Uganda · Tanzania · Zambia · Sierra Leone · GiveWell · Founders Pledge · Gavi · 44 LLOs

KMC

Kangaroo Mother Care

Closing the post-discharge gap for small or vulnerable newborns. Up to 40% reduction in neonatal mortality; global coverage below 5%; 4–5 structured home visits per case.

Uganda · Kenya · India · Nigeria · Eleanor Crook Foundation · 4,005+ cases (March 2026)

ECD

Early Childhood Development

76 FLWs, 9,510+ visits, 50-caregiver baseline/endline. Knowledge moved 33–41 points. Behavior moved 21 points. Attitudes were already correct.

Malawi · Nigeria · Mozambique · Founders Pledge · 27,000+ visits to date

Readers

Vision Care / Reading Glasses

RestoringVision partnership for 15,000 reading-glasses pairs to presbyopic adults. Verified-payment model applied to a non-clinical commodity.

Nigeria · RestoringVision · LLOs being finalized · contract signing pending

MBW

Mother-Baby Wellness

Maternal-health program in active deployment; public depth page pending.

Multiple regions · GiveWell

Chlorine

Chlorine Dispenser

Connect's verified-payment model applied to water treatment. $1M GiveWell grant; pre-deployment.

Nigeria · GiveWell · launching 2026

WellMe

Mental Health

Mental-health screening and supportive counseling at the community level.

Uganda · pilot

RUTF

Therapeutic Food

Ready-to-use therapeutic food distribution; new $1M CIFF grant.

Multiple regions · CIFF · launching 2026

Interviews

Survey & Data Collection

Connect adapted for verified survey collection rather than visit delivery — a research instrument.

Multiple regions · GiveWell · $225k

Rooftop

Rooftop Sampling

Environmental-health sampling protocol on the Connect verified-visit rail.

Multiple regions · pilot
