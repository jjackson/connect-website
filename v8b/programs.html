Active · Evidence-backed program files

Four programs with their own essays.

Child health campaigns

CHC

Nutrition screening (MUAC), vaccination, treatment referral. 44 LLOs, 880K+ verified visits in 14 months. The program where most of Connect's insights were first measured — microplanning, adversarial fraud testing, Trial Run model, cost inversion, and the CAR failure we published.

 Kangaroo mother care

KMC

Five structured home visits per small-and-vulnerable newborn. Aligned to the WHO 2022 guideline update. Active in India. Live live number: 50% against a 70% target on the 3-day-post-discharge guardrail, and what we're doing about it.

 Early childhood development

ECD

Responsive caregiving, talk-and-play, behavioral rehearsal. Malawi pilot moved knowledge +33-41% and observed behavior +21%. Reshaping the intervention — from 3 visits to 8-10 visits across 12 months.

 Reading glasses

Readers

Presbyopia screening and distribution with Restoring Vision. The first non-child-health sector on Connect. An honest test of whether the architecture generalizes — evidence still building.
